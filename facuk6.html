Factory Communications UK (Part 6: FAC 251 to 300)

FAC 251
New Factory Headquarters / Postcard / Party
Sep 90

Additional Notes:

Catalogue number applies to the new headquarters, a postcard thereon, and items from its grand opening such as the party entry pass, and a very rare CD/invitation (see below) to the Headquarters grand opening.

For important releases the whole building was plastered with 60"x40" posters for the release. The building was acquired in October 1988, officially opened on 29 September 1990 and closed November 1992.
