Additional Notes:

"The first official Crepuscule release was the legendary 'From Brussels With Love compilation, that got allocated catalogue number 007, and was released in November 1980. But during the months before Crepuscule had already been around, existing in very close relationship with the Factory Benelux label, set up by Factory boss Wilson and Michel Duval, a Belgian journalist. In effect, the first couple of Factory Benelux records were Crepuscule releases at the same time: FAC BN 1 = TWI 004, FAC BN 2 = TWI 005 & FAC BN 3 = TWI 006 ('Crepuscule' is French for 'Twilight', hence the TWI...)." - Frank Brinkhuis
